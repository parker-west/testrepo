About Me

Hi, I'm Phillip Parker and this is my newly built "Basic Porfolio" website. It is part of a project assigned throught the University of Texas via the Coding Bootcamp program.


Originally from Hastings, Nebraska, I attended college in Lincoln (Nebraska's capital) for two years. From there, I went soul searching and attended the Musician's Institute in Hollywood, Califonia for a year where I studied percussion and contempory drums. Music school was an amazing and formative experience; however, obtaining a professional career in music with any success seemed nearly impossible. Discovering that achieving that status of a "starving artist" was no longer appealing to me (not sure if it ever was), I finished my degree at California State University of Long Beach with degrees in Journalism and Entrepreneurship.
